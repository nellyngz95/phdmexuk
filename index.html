What Happened

A Crisis Hidden in Plain Sight

In 2022, Fundación Politécnico and Instituto Politécnico Nacional made a partnership with UK Universities. The program consisted in giving excellence students a 4-year PhD program covering fees and a stipend.

On March 23rd, 2026, researchers recieved a notification via mail from Fundacion Politécnico stating that their funding will be suspended for temporarily and preventive reasons. PhD MEX UK exists to make this visible — and to hold those responsible to account.

Payments Stopped Without Notice

Stipends were cut abruptly, leaving researchers without income overnight — no warning, no timeline, no explanation given.

No Emergency Support Provided

UK universities and CONAHCYT failed to put any emergency financial support in place for the hundreds of researchers affected.

Visas & Legal Status at Risk

Without income, many researchers face the threat of losing their visa status — putting their entire academic future in jeopardy.

No Accountability So Far

No institution (Fundación politécnico and Instituto Politécnico Nacional) has formally accepted responsibility. We are demanding transparency and urgent remediation.
