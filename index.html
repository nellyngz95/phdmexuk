What Happened

A Crisis Hidden in Plain Sight

In 2022, Fundación Politécnico and Instituto Politécnico Nacional formed a partnership with UK universities — offering excellence students a fully-funded 4-year PhD programme covering fees and a monthly stipend.

On March 23rd, 2026, researchers received a notification from Fundación Politécnico stating that their funding would be suspended for 'temporary and preventive reasons' — with no timeline, no support plan, and no direct communication from IPN. PhD MEX UK exists to make this visible — and to hold those responsible to account.

Payments Stopped Without Notice

Stipends were abruptly suspended, leaving researchers without income overnight — no warning and no timeline. Fundación Politécnico cited a breakdown between them and IPN in 2025 as the reason.

An Uncertain Future

UK universities and SECIHTI, with support from the Mexican Embassy in the UK, are trying to find solutions. But neither Fundación Politécnico nor the IPN has formally responded.

Visas & Legal Status at Risk

Without income, many researchers face the threat of losing their visa status — putting their entire academic future in jeopardy.

No Accountability So Far

Neither Fundación Politécnico nor the Instituto Politécnico Nacional has formally accepted responsibility. We are demanding transparency and urgent remediation.

Cronology

How Did We Get Here?

From the IPN internationalisation programme to the funding suspension — a timeline of key events.

2022 · CDMX

Partnership

The Call

Fundación Politécnico and IPN launched a call for excellence students to join a fully-funded 4-year PhD programme at UK universities, covering tuition fees and a monthly stipend.

2023 · London

Honorary Doctorate

Students Informed of Underpayment

The IPN Director received an honorary doctorate from the UK university partner. Students were not permitted to attend — instead they were called to a meeting with Fundación Politécnico, where they were told their stipend covered only half of the UK legal minimum.

Summer 2024 · London

Symposium

First Mexican Symposium

Students from the first two cohorts were asked to staff and present their research. The IPN General Director and Fundación Politécnico signed new contracts with the UK university — but held no meeting with the PhD students themselves.

2025 · London

Scholarship Revoked

A Student Loses Their Funding

One student's scholarship was revoked with no formal communication or response from IPN or Fundación Politécnico, despite submitting all required documentation.

23 Mar 2026 · UK

Funding Suspended

The Crisis Begins

All 39 researchers receive an email from Fundación Politécnico stating their funding is suspended 'temporarily and preventively' — citing the breakdown of contracts between IPN and Fundación in 2025. No timeline, no support, no plan.
